Questions

How we work

Are there any guarantees?
Yes, we provide clear project milestones and deliverables. Our track record speaks for itself — we've successfully delivered projects for over 4 years with transparent communication and documented results.
Why Ruby on Rails?
Ruby on Rails enables rapid development with its convention-over-configuration approach and vast ecosystem of libraries. It allows us to build scalable, maintainable applications quickly — ideal for both MVPs and complex enterprise solutions.
Do you work from a specification?
We work based on detailed technical specifications that we create together with you. This ensures clear requirements, accurate timelines, and predictable results. If you don't have one yet, we can help you build it.
What does the typical timeline look like?
Small MVPs can be delivered in 1 week. Medium-complexity projects typically take 1–2 months. Complex systems are scoped and delivered in iterative phases with regular milestones and demos.
